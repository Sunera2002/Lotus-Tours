Top Accommodations in Kandy

Earl's Regency Hotel is a luxurious 5-star hotel located a 5 km from Kandy Railway Station. It offers spacious accommodation with a spa, an outdoor pool and free parking.

Attractively situated in the centre of Kandy, Grand Serendib Hotel features air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, free private parking and room service.

Featuring air conditioning, Ru Boutique is located in Kandy, 200 metres from Kandy Museum. Sri Dalada Maligawa is 300 metres from the property. The accommodation is fitted with a flat-screen TV.


The Grand Kandyan

The Grand Kandyan, is a 5-star property, tucked away in Kandy only 1.2 km from Sri Dalada Maligawa. It welcomes guests with a spacious outdoor pool and complimentary WiFi access.
